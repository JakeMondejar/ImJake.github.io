Discovering Digital Art
My passion for art truly took off when I encountered digital art during my studies at Father Saturnino University. Enrolled in an art subject, I learned the fundamentals of 2D animation and 3D modeling. This experience sparked my obsession with the limitless possibilities of digital design.
How I Spend My Time
When I'm not working on my designs, you can usually find me creating 3D models, hanging out on Discord, or indulging in my love for video games and movies. I'm also always on the lookout for the latest tech to get my hands on, as I enjoy staying up-to-date with new innovations.
